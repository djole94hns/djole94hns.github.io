About us

Apartment Iva, beautiful small duplex. It can house 3 people. It has a living room, a separate bedroom, a kitchen and a bathroom with a walk-in shower. There is central heating, WiFi, cable TV, clean bedding and towels, washing machine, hair dryer, iron, eating utensils, dishwasher. There's free parking. The apartment's location will suit anyone who's looking for a stay of any duration. The apartment is near two hospitality complexes Kamenjar i Ribarsko ostrvo (Ribarac). In the elite weekend destination Kamenjar are restaurants Bata Pezo, Čarda Mačak, Čarda Cesla... At Ribarac, we recommend the restaurant of the same name, Ribarac, restaurant Piknik, float Ski bar, Yachting club Skipper... Throwing house parties is not allowed. Dogs are allowed, and a bed, toilet and food bowls are provided for them. ?? The street is calm, and there's a beautiful riverside walkway nearby. Transport to the city is good, the bus stop is a couple of steps away. It takes about 10 minutes to get to the city center by foot.
